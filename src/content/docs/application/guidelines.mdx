---
title: Application guidelines
---

- We're a whitelisted server. That means that you need to apply to join us.
- We do this to make sure that everyone on the server is a good fit for our
  community, and to keep the server safe and fun for everyone who plays!
- We also need to check everyone has the best of intentions when they join, and
  that they understand our rules.

## Before you start

- It's important that you [read our rules](/rules) before you apply!
- You'll also need to have a paid Minecraft account to join - we aren't a
  cracked server, so you won't be able to join with a free launcher,
  unfortunately.
- We also need a basic level of English so you're able to understand the rules
  and communicate with other players and understand staff messages!

## How to apply

- Join [our discord server](https://discord.gg/tbnXCufv2B).
- Read the [rules](/rules) and make sure you understand them and haven't missed
  anything :)
- Read through this application guide to make sure you are making a good
  application.
- Go to the #tickets channel, and click on "Make a server application" to get
  started!

## How much should you write?

- We don't have a word count requirement.
- We do want to make sure you are going to be a great, long-term player for
  our server!
- So the more you can tell us about yourself, the better! We want to get to know
  you and understand why you'd be a good fit for our community.
- As a general rule of thumb, you don't need to write more than the Discord
  character limit (2000 characters)!
- You can do if you want though, and we'd greatly encourage you to if you have
  more to say about yourself!

## A tight-knit community

- We try to be a small server where everyone can get to know each other and make
  friends.
- We want to make sure that everyone is a good applicant and will benefit the
  experience of other players.
- To do that, it means we have to be strict on applications. Not everyone who
  applies will be accepted, and we do have to reject applications that don't
  meet our standards.
- We understand that this can be disappointing, but we hope you understand that
  we're trying to create the best possible experience for everyone on the
  server!
- To have the best chance of being accepted, make sure you read the rules and this guide
  carefully, and write a great application that tells us about yourself and why
  you'd be a great fit for our community. It's not all about how much you write,
  but the more you can tell us about yourself and why you'd be a great fit for
  our community, the better!

## Starting your application

- If you're struggling to begin the application process, please do ask! We'd be
  more than happy to
- Please follow the rest of this guide to make sure you can write a great
  application and convince us that you're a great fit for our community.
- You do not need to follow everything in the guide, and you are very welcome to
  write your application in your own style and include anything you want to tell
  us (in fact, we encourage you to).

## What if I don't want to apply?

- If you don't want to apply, that's totally fine! We understand that not
  everyone will want to join our server, and that's okay!
- We hope you have a great time playing Minecraft, whether that's on our server
  or on another server that better suits your interests and playstyle.
- If you'd like to join *our* community, you'll need to apply and be accepted.
- We don't accept everyone who applies. We have to be strict on applications to
  make sure we are creating the best possible experience for everyone on the
  server!
- That means we unfortunately have to reject applications that don't meet our
  standards.
- Please do put in as much effort as you can into your application! It means you
  will be much more likely to be accepted, and it also helps the other server
  members get to know you if you choose to share your application!
