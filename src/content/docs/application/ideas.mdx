---
title: Ideas for your application
---

Not sure what to write in your application? Here are some great starting points
you make want to write about.

Including some (or all!) of these things in your application will make your
application much stronger and likely to be accepted.

## Things you need to include

- Your Minecraft username.

## What you may like to say about yourself

- Your timezone and when you're usually online.
- Any hobbies you're interested in, related or unrelated to Minecraft.
- Do you have, or plan to invite, any friends on/to the server?
- Anything else about your personality or experiences that you'd like to share
  with us!
  - This could include your job, what you study at school/college, your music
    tastes, any cool things you've done in or outside of Minecraft, or anything
    else you'd be interested in sharing with us!

## Why do you want to join Vanillacraft?

- This is a really important question!
- It helps us understand why you want to join our community, and what you hope to get
  out of the experience.
- It also helps us understand whether you have a good understanding of what our
  server is about, and whether you're a good fit for our community.
- You can talk about what you like about our server, what you hope to do on the
  server, and anything else you'd like to share about why you want to join us.
- We also want to get any expectations you have about the server, so we can make
  sure we can meet them - this is a chance to make sure *we* are the right server
  for *you* just as much as it's a chance for you to show us you're the right
  player for us!

## Your plans for the server

- This is your chance to tell us about what you hope to do on the server!
- Do you want to build big things? Do you want to explore and find cool places?
  Do you want to make friends and be a part of the community? Or anything else!
- We want to get a sense of what you hope to do on the server, and how you hope
  to contribute.
- It also helps us know whether this server is for you or whether you might be
  better off on a different server with a different focus. We want to make sure
  that everyone on the server is going to have a great time and get what they
  want out of the server!

## How will you contribute to our community?

- This section is also something that's really important to include!
- It's your chance to show us how you will be a positive member of our community, and how
  you will contribute to the experience of other players on the server.
- You can talk about how you will be friendly and helpful to other players, how you
  will follow the rules and set a good example for others, and anything else you'd
  like to share about how you will contribute to our community!
- We want to make sure that everyone on the server is a positive influence on the
  community, and that everyone is contributing to making the server a fun and
  enjoyable place for everyone to play!
- This is your chance to show us that you will be a great member of our
  community, and it's one of the best ways to convince us we should accept your
  application.
