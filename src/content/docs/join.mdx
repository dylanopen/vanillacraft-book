- Vanillacraft is a whitelisted Minecraft server.
- That means you need to be approved before you can start playing.
- Don't worry: it's nothing complicated - we simply ask new members to tell us
  a little bit about themselves, and our staff team reviews applications to make
  sure the server stays a friendly and welcoming place for everyone.

## How to start playing

There are a few simple steps to do before you start:

<Steps>

1. Join our [Discord server](https://discord.gg/EFygPtvrvn) and say hello.
2. Read our server rules and guidelines, which you can find in this book
   (under the [Rules](/rules) section). Make sure you have read them carefully:
   we have our ways of knowing if you have ;\)
3. Make an application ticket in our Discord server. You can find that in the
   #tickets channel, or see [Making a ticket](/ticket/create) for help.
4. Read through our [application ideas](/join/ideas) to get some ideas for
   things you may want to include in your application! You have a **lot** of
   freedom with your application, so feel free to be creative and show us who 
   you are if you'd rather not use the guide!
5. Wait for a staff member to review your application. We try to review
   applications as quickly as possible, but it may take up to or (occasionally)
   more than a day. Please be patient - we will get to it as soon as we can!
6. If your application is accepted, you can join as soon as you link your 
   Discord account to your Minecraft account! If you need help with that, see
   [Linking your account](/join/link).
7. You can start playing! We'll give you the server IP and any other information
   you ask for - and we'd be happy to show you around the server with a tour if
   you ask for one when joining. See [Joining the Minecraft server](
   /join/minecraft) for more information on how to join a Minecraft server.

</Steps>
