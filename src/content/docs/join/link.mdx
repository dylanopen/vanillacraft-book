As soon as your application has been accepted, you'll be able to link your
Minecraft and Discord accounts together, which will allow you to join the server
and start playing!

## Steps

<Steps>

1. Go to the `#🔗-get-whitelisted-🔗` channel on our discord server.
2. Click the `🔗 Link Account!` button in that channel, which will open a box
   asking for your Minecraft username.
3. Enter your Minecraft username and click `Submit`.
4. You should get a confirmation message that your accounts have been linked,
   and you can now join the server!
5. Use the `#🕹️-server-ip-🕹️` channel to join the server! If you need help with
   that, see [Joining the Minecraft server](/join/minecraft).

</Steps>
