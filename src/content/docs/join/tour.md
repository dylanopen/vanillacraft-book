---
title: Getting a tour
---

Once you've [joined Vanillacraft for the first time](/join/how), you may well
want to be shown around the server!

Often, one of our staff members will be around to give you a tour, but if not,
you can ask for one in-game and another server member will usually be happy to
show you around!

If you want a tour, just say something like "Hi, I'm new! Would somebody be able
to show me around?" in the Minecraft chat.

## Tours from staff members

If you'd like a tour from a staff member, you may have to wait a little while
until someone is free.

> If you're able to join the server as soon as you are whitelisted, then a staff
> is more likely to be available!
