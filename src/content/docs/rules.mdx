This is a non-exhaustive summary of the most important rules and guidelines you
need to follow when playing on Vanillacraft or talking in our community.

You can find out more information about each rule by clicking on the card.

> Staff members have the final say in any situation, and may take action if they
> see it necessary to enforce these rules, or any other rules which make sense
> in the situation.

## Rules

<LinkCard
  title="Respect your fellow Vanillacrafters"
  href="/rules/respect"
  description="Be nice to other player, don't overstep boundaries."
/>

<LinkCard
  title="Don't damage players' builds"
  href="/rules/griefing"
  description="Don't break blocks other players have placed, or cause damage to their builds."
/>

<LinkCard
  title="Don't steal from shops or bases"
  href="/rules/stealing"
  description="Don't take items without paying, or steal from other players' bases. Whatever you may think, we *always* notice - it's not worth it :)"
/>

<LinkCard
  title="PVP is optional"
  href="/rules/pvp"
  description="Don't attack other players without their permission. If you have permission, go ahead and have fun!"
/>

<LinkCard
  title="What sort of mods are OK?"
  href="/rules/mods"
  description="Most mods are fine, except 'cheat' mods giving an advantage, like hacked clients, xray (including texture packs), and others. Litematica is allowed, its 'printer' is not."
/>

<LinkCard
  title="Keeping the chat clean"
  href="/rules/chat"
  description="We don't allow excessive swearing in Minecraft and Discord chat. Please add the phrase 'little cube' in your application to show you have read the rules. Any sort of NSFW messages or content is strictly forbidden."
/>

<LinkCard
  title="Advertising"
  href="/rules/advertising"
  description="Please don't advertise anything external, unless you have made a ticket and have admin permission."
/>

<LinkCard
  title="Invite your friends!"
  href="/rules/inviting"
  description="It's the best way we can grow the server, and having a friend to play along with you on the server is always more fun than playing alone :)"
/>
