---
title: Keep the chat clean
---

- We don't allow excessive swearing in Minecraft and Discord chat. We won't take action against occasional bad language, but ideally, just don't use negative words :)
- Any sort of NSFW messages or content is strictly forbidden, and will result in a ban from the discord and minecraft server.
- This is not the place for political discussion and debates.
- Sharing experiences which were shaped by societal issues is not itself politics. Discussing that here could help people.
