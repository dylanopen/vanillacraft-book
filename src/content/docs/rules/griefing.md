---
title: Don't damage players' builds
---

- Any sort of griefing isn't tolerated here.
- Breaking any block somebody else placed is griefing (within reason).
- Setting fires, explosions, 'accidentally' destroying something someone worked hard on, is included.
- Did someone damage your base? Speak to them about it, or ask staff to roll it back in a ticket!

Please do let us know if your build is damaged. We can roll back any damage and
take action against the player if you report it to us!
