---
title: Inviting your friends
---

- This one isn't really a 'rule', but it's the best way we can grow the server!
- Also, having a friend to play along with you on the server is always more fun than playing alone (even if you're meeting new people).
- Also, look out for the events we host! They're often a great way to meet new people and find common interests.
- And if you are playing alone - we hope you find someone here who shares your interests and you can get to know them!
