---
title: What mods are allowed?
---


- Most mods are fine on Vanillacraft!
- Fullbright, freecam, Xaero's minimap and performance mods like Sodium are all mods we recommend!
- However, we don't allow 'cheat' mods giving an advantage, like:
- Hacked clients, xray (including texture packs), and others.
- Litematica is allowed, except for the 'printer' mode.
- We do frequently monitor if people are using cheats, and have anticheat software to make sure the server is fair for everyone!

> Please ask staff if you are unsure whether a mod is allowed.
