---
title: PVP is optional
---

- We have a consent system to PVP on Vanillacraft.
- If you and a friend agree to PVP - go ahead!
- However, please do not attack other players if they have not given you permission.
- We do enforce this - if you repeatedly attack other players without permission, we may take action.
- Again though, if another player is annoying you - let us know and we'll sort it out!

> Everybody should have the right to play the game peacefully and without being
> attacked by other players. Respect this! Other people may not enjoy the same
> in-game activities as you do.
