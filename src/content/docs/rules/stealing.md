---
title: Don't steal from shops or bases
---

- Items in a shop are sold for a specific price. So don't take items without paying!
- Underpaying in shops is the same. Please don't do it.
- It's not just shops: stealing from others' bases isn't allowed - this isn't anarchy - we're friendly here!
- If you think we won't notice... we will ;)
- Think you've been stolen from? Make a stealing ticket and we'll investigate and get you your stuff back!

> We have a custom-coded plugin to alert staff whenever a player takes items
> from shops or chests. We *always* know when a player has stolen something, and
> it simply is not worth it to steal from other players.  

*Please listen to this advice...* we have a zero-tolerance policy on stealing.
