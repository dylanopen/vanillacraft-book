title: Apply for staff
---

The best way you can help out Vanillacraft is by giving some of your time to
help out server members. The most effective way to do that is as a staff member
of our server!

## How to apply

You can apply for staff by making a `Staff Application` ticket in our Discord
server.

> Not sure how to make a ticket? See [Making a ticket](/ticket/create) for help.

## Requirements

While these aren't *strict* requirements (we may still consider you if you don't
meet one of them!) we do look for these things in staff applicants.

A great staff member has many qualities, for example:

- Has played on the server for at least **one month**.
- Has been active most of the time throughout their time on the server.
- Is well-liked by the community.
- Hasn't been banned from the server in the past.
- Is not subject to a previous server controversy.
- Is respectful to all our players (and follows [our rules](/rules)).
- Does not use staff permissions to their advantage.
- Does not engage in unnecessary arguments with the community.

## What you may like to include in your application

Here are some questions that may help us get to know you as a staff member!

> You don't have to write about all of these - just anything you feel like.

- Tell us a bit about yourself.
- What can you bring to our community?
- Do you have any ideas for changes to the server you'd like to make?
- Do you have past moderation experience on other servers?
- Any Minecraft-specific technical knowledge (e.g. plugin development, server
  management, etc)?
- What role specifically are you looking for (e.g. `Staff`, `Administrator`,
  `Developer`, `Builder`, `Event Lead`, etc)?
