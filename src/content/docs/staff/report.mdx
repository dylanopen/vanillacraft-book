---
title: Reporting staff behaviour
---

If you don't think that a staff acted professionally in response to an issue,
please tell us!

Our administrator team (higherup staff) will review the situation, and if we
believe that one of our staff members acted in the wrong way, we'll talk to
them about it.

## Our review process

We review staff applications carefully, and only accept those from people who
are already trusted in the Vanillacraft community.

This means that it's unlikely you'll have issues with our staff team, but of
course, it is still a possibility, and so we give the option of reporting a
member of the staff team confidentially to our administrator team, if you ever
need to.

> Please note that we take all reports seriously, and will review them as soon
> as we can. However, false reports or reports made in bad faith may be ignored,
> and may lead to consequences for the person making the report.

## Reporting a staff member

To report a staff member, please [open a ticket](/ticket/create) in our Discord
server, in the `Report a Staff Member` category.

We'll review this as soon as we can.

> Please note that administration tickets can take longer to review than other
> types of tickets. See [Ticket response times](/ticket/time) for more
> information.

## Confidentiality

Only the administrator team will have access to the report, and we will not 
share the report with the staff member in question until we have reviewed it and
decided on the best course of action.

You can find a list of server administrators in the [Staff](/staff) section of
this book.
