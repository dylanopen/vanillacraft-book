There are a few reasons you might want to make a ticket on our discord server:
one of which being... you need to make an application ticket to join in the
first place!

## Steps

To make a ticket:

<Steps>

1. Go to the [`🎫-tickets-🎫`](https://discord.com/channels/1370146934685175964/1416889162921021612)
channel in our Discord server.
2. Choose the type of ticket you want to make, by clicking on the right button
   / dropdown.
3. Enter the information requested in the orm, if applicable.
4. You **must** then write **all the details** which can be used to answer the
   question / help you. Do this as a single message in the newly created ticket.
   If you don't provide enough information about the issue,
   we may close the ticket and ask you to make a new one with more information.
5. Wait for a staff member to respond to your ticket. We try to respond as
   quickly as possible, but it may take up to or (occasionally) more than a day.
   Please be patient - we will get to it as soon as we can!

</Steps>


> See the [ticket response times](/ticket/time) page for more information on
> how long it may take to get a response, for each ticket type.
